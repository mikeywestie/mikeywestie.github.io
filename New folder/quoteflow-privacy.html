QuoteFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14 June 2026

QuoteFlow Mobile helps users create, manage, and organize quotations and related business information.

Information Stored

QuoteFlow stores quotation data, customer information, product information, templates, and application settings entered by the user.

Data Collection

QuoteFlow does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with third parties.

All information is stored locally on the user's device unless the user explicitly exports or shares data.

Third-Party Services

QuoteFlow does not use advertising networks or third-party analytics services.

Data Security

Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices.

Children's Privacy

QuoteFlow is intended for business and productivity purposes and is not directed at children under the age of 13.
